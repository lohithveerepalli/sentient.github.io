About Sentient

Discover the world of META VR and our Zen App for meditative experiences.

Zen: Your Gateway to Tranquility

Zen is not just an app; it's a transformative experience that takes you on a serene journey into the heart of meditation. Crafted meticulously for VR, Zen redefines the meditative process, offering an unparalleled immersive experience that elevates your mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Immersive Meditation Environments

Imagine meditating in a tranquil Zen garden, beside a gently flowing stream, or atop a serene mountain at sunrise. Zen transports you to these calming locales and more, all rendered in stunningly realistic VR. Each setting is designed to engage your senses and promote deep relaxation and mindfulness.

Guided Sessions for All Levels

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, Zen has something for everyone. Choose from a wide range of guided meditation sessions led by world-class experts. These sessions cover various techniques, from mindful breathing to progressive relaxation, ensuring that every meditation journey is both enriching and accessible.

Personalized Meditation Journeys

Zen understands that meditation is a personal experience. That's why we offer customizable sessions. Adjust the duration, environment, and guidance level to suit your individual preferences, making each meditation session uniquely yours.

Mindful Movement Practices

Zen takes a holistic approach to wellness by incorporating mindful movement practices like gentle yoga and Tai Chi. These sessions, designed specifically for VR, help you to harmonize your body and mind, enhancing the meditative experience.
